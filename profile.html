Portfolio Website

A portfolio is a collection of your work that showcases your skills, projects, and experience. It acts like an online resume but is more detailed and interactive. Introduction – A short description of who you are and what you do. Projects – Examples of websites or applications you have built, with links and descriptions. Skills – The technologies you are proficient in, such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. Contact Information – Ways for people to reach you, like email, LinkedIn, or GitHub.

To-Do App

A To-Do List App is a simple application that helps users organize tasks by listing things they need to do. It usually includes features like: Adding tasks – Write down what needs to be done. Marking tasks as complete – Tick off finished tasks. Deleting tasks Remove tasks that are no longer needed. Editing tasks Modify tasks if plans change. Sorting tasks Arrange by priority, due date, etc..
